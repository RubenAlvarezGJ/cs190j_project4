# fLoW

## Enemies — Zombies

- **Zombies are local to each area** and **escalate in difficulty as you progress** (more numerous, faster, and tougher in later areas). This is the backbone of the Flow Channel.
- **Each area is gated by a survival/wave objective**: clear a set number of zombies (or survive a set number of rounds) before the exit unlocks. Progression is therefore *dependent on player success*.
- Difficulty can ramp *within* an area across its waves, so each location has its own mini flow curve nested inside the overall escalation.
- **No cross-area persistence** — each area owns and manages its own zombies, so leaving one area does not drag enemies into the next.

**Soft-lock safeguard:** the player must always have a recovery path during waves (backpack heals, forest food) so a gate can't trap an underpowered or out-of-healing player. Tune wave size with this in mind.

## Tutorial Level — *low skill / low difficulty*

- Pickup pickaxe
- Pickup backpack
- Learn to mine
- Learn to attack
- **A few slow shambler zombies** to safely teach swinging/attacking
- Survival gate: clear a small number of zombies to proceed

## Factory

- Find some coal but can't carry it yet because no usable inventory
- Find some leather for a backpack
- Go look around, eventually find some string and straps
- **Unlock building** by finding different parts of a backpack and feeding them to a sewing machine — spits out a backpack
- Backpack allows you to hold materials in order to build, items, and heals
- Go back and pick up the coal — "it'll be important later!"
- **Zombies:** slightly more numerous than the tutorial; a small wave gate before the exit
- Hop on the bus and see where it takes you

## Military Watch Tower / Shrine / Forest

- **Military Watch Tower** — explore and find some TNT (but can't ignite it), pick it up and use later
- Explore the outside and find a path you can follow into the forest which leads you to a shrine
- **Shrine** has katana and samurai armor which can be equipped immediately
- **Forest** for scenery, can find heals (food)
- **Zombies:** tougher/faster here; the katana + armor unlock is positioned so the player gears up *before* the area's difficulty spike — gear unlock lands just ahead of the wave gate
- Hop on daa buss

## Village

- **Unlock smithing** by putting together a crafting table — allows you to upgrade your weapon and shields at a price (needs the coal from the Factory to be powered; later will be powered by electricity from the next POI)
- **Zombies:** harder waves that genuinely require the katana/armor and upgraded gear; survival gate before progressing

## Water Dam — *high skill / high difficulty*

- **Unlock power** across the map by restoring a water dam — destroy boulder with explosive items you found at the Watch Tower and some matches you find here
- **Zombies:** hardest encounters in the game — the climax wave that tests everything the player has learned and built; final survival gate

---

## Flow Channel (overall difficulty curve)

Tutorial (lowest) → Factory → Watch Tower/Shrine/Forest → Village → Water Dam (highest).
Each step escalates zombie count/speed/toughness, each area is gated on player success (wave/survival objective), and key gear/system unlocks (backpack, katana+armor, smithing) are placed just ahead of difficulty spikes so the player is equipped to meet each rise.

## Overall Story / Progression Systems

1. Unlock Building and item carrying
2. Unlock Smithing
3. Restore Power
